Chapters & Visual Content

Summary

Concept features for Spotify to give more control to Podcast Creators.

Chapters allows creators to easily divide their content into sections to help listeners better navigate and enjoy their podcasts.

Visual Content allows creators to make their podcasts more immersive and engaging with pictures, quotes and links to resources referenced in the show.

Disclaimer: This project had no affiliation with Spotify AB, but a variation of the Chapters feature has since been implemented in the actual Spotify app.
