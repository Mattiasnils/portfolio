Summary

A concept feature for iMessage with the purpose of eliciting nostalgic emotions through a spatiotemporal experience. The feature enables the user to explore their past conversations as they occurred in the digital and physical hybrid space by placing all messages on a map for the user to explore. Weaving together messages, location data and pictures from the camera roll creates an immersive nostaligic experience.
