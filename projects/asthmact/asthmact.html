asthmact

Predicting asthma symptoms

Summary

Through a course in Creating New Business, myself and a multi-disciplinary group of students founded a fictional tech startup to help asthmatics predict their asthma symptoms based on personal triggers and exercise data. I performed Market Analysis, Ethnographic Research, and a Co-design Workshop with asthmatic athletes as our target audience. I collaborated in building a Business Plan and Customer Acquisition Strategy, collaborated in building a Landing Page, produced posters and designed Lo-fi Prototypes and Hi-fi Mockups of the app UI. The concept was iterated and evaluated using two rounds of Customer Feedback.
