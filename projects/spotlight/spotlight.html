Summary

Spotlight is a mobile platform for connecting people based on activities, common interest and individual preferences.

User research was carried out through a Focus Group to elicit needs and understand attitudes towards social events and online match-making. Insights were analyzed using Affinity Diagrams. Ideation led to three Wireflow Prototypes that were evaluated through User Journeys. The team proceeded to create interactive Figma Prototypes, Brand Identity and iterate the design through Think Aloud Walkthroughs with potential users. The project resulted in a concept iOS app where the user can select an activity, adjust preferences, and then enter a lobby to wait for more people joining. When the lobby is complete, a group is automatically created and participants can chat to decide on the details and meet up.
