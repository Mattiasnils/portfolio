Teamlink Designer

Summary

Individual project with the goal of reinventing and facilitating remote creative collaboration. The solution combines a sophisticated creative workspace and application switcher with a neat meeting manager. I experimented with several interaction ideas through low- to mid-fidelity prototypes and finally put everything together in a high-fidelity mockup complete with detailed micro-interactions and branding.

Year

2020

Duration

4 weeks
