Summary

Designed and engineered a part light-based, part tablet-based user interface for autonomous transport vehicles. Employed an iterative, human-centered design process involving extensive Ethnographic Research at Volvo, Zenseact and SKF. Developed and tested Interactive Prototypes in After Effects and Adobe XD. The resulting guidelines and concept helped improve situational awareness, trust and safety for passengers and surrounding traffic in controlled environments.
