About

I'm an ambitious, impact-seeking designer and engineer specialized in the intersection between human behavior and technology. I am driven by curiosity towards people and how powerful technology can be made accessible, intuitive and delightful to everyone through user-centered design.

As a designer, I advocate for rigorous user research and testing in order to yield products and services that comply with user needs and exceeds expectations. My goal is to develop simple solutions to complex problems, taking into account everything from users and technology to business and sustainability goals.

As a person, I'm open-minded, initiative-taking and have a positive, can-do attitude. I'm always eager to learn and collaborate in teams that embrace diverse perspectives.
